Dota Tracker

About The Site:

Dota Tracker tracks various bits of data about Valve's game Dota 2.
It keeps records of players based on their Steam_IDs, matches based on their Match IDs, and player statistics with the heroes they have played.
For a better version of this, go to dotabuff.com
